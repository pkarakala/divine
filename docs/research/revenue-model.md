# Divine — Revenue Model & Financial Projections

## Revenue Strategy

Divine uses a **subscription-first** model with supplementary in-app purchases and events revenue. Modeled after Raya's simplicity with Hinge's tiered approach.

---

## Pricing Structure

### Subscription Tiers

| Tier | Price | Features |
|---|---|---|
| **Free** | $0 | 5 likes/day, basic matching, limited profile views |
| **Divine+** | $14.99/month | Unlimited likes, see who liked you, advanced org filters, read receipts |
| **Divine Elite** | $29.99/month | Everything in +, priority matching, profile boost/week, event discounts, "Most Compatible" daily pick |

### Why This Pricing
- **Free tier** exists to build density — new members need to match immediately
- **$14.99** undercuts Hinge ($30) while still netting ~$12.75 after Apple's 15% (Small Business Program)
- **$29.99** matches premium dating apps; nets ~$25.50 after fees
- Once over $1M revenue, Apple takes 30% → net $10.50/$21 respectively → may need to raise prices or push annual plans

### Annual Plan Discounts
- Divine+: $99.99/year (save 44%) → $8.33/month effective
- Divine Elite: $199.99/year (save 44%) → $16.67/month effective
- **Why:** Annual subscribers trigger Apple's 15% rate after Year 1 auto-renew. Also dramatically improves retention.

### Weekly Plans (Testing Phase)
- Divine+ Weekly: $5.99/week → $25.96/month effective (premium for flexibility)
- 47% of Social/Lifestyle apps use weekly plans (RevenueCat 2026 data)
- Higher effective revenue but lower commitment barrier

## App Store Economics

### Apple App Store Fees
| Revenue Stage | Commission | Net per $14.99 sub | Net per $29.99 sub |
|---|---|---|---|
| Under $1M (Small Business Program) | 15% | $12.74 | $25.49 |
| Over $1M (Year 1 subscribers) | 30% | $10.49 | $20.99 |
| Over $1M (Year 2+ subscribers) | 15% | $12.74 | $25.49 |

### Google Play Store Fees
| Type | Commission | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| All subscriptions | 15% | Regardless of revenue level |
| Other in-app purchases (first $1M) | 15% | Then 30% above $1M |

### Implication
- Google Play is more developer-friendly for subscriptions (always 15%)
- Push annual subscriptions on iOS to trigger the 15% rate sooner
- Under $1M revenue (Year 1), both platforms are 15%

## Funding Strategy

### Bootstrapped Phase (Year 0-1)
- Personal investment: $10K-$30K (covers infrastructure, design, Apple Developer fee)
- Revenue covers operational costs by Q3-Q4
- No VC needed if Pranav builds the product himself

### Optional: Seed Round (Year 1-2)
- If accelerating growth: raise $500K-$1M seed
- Use for: marketing at conventions, hiring 1-2 engineers, event operations
- Valuation basis: 10-15x ARR = $8.6M-$13M at end of Year 1

### Growth Round (Year 2-3)
- If going aggressive: raise $3-5M Series A
- Use for: national expansion, matchmaking service, brand partnerships
- Valuation basis: 10-15x ARR = $49M-$74M at end of Year 2

### Exit Scenarios (Year 4-5+)
1. **Acquisition by Match Group** — they already own BLK; Divine fills a premium niche. Comp: The League acquisition (2022). Potential: $50-100M+
2. **Standalone growth** — continue as profitable niche business at $15-30M ARR
3. **Expand TAM** — open to broader HBCU alumni, Black professional networks → $50M+ ARR potential
